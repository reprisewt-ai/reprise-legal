Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 18, 2026

RepRise ("we", "our", or "us") operates the RepRise AI Workout Tracker mobile application (the "App"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

Account Data
When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials, managed via Firebase Authentication (Google LLC).

Workout & Training Data
We collect exercise logs, workout routines, sets, reps, weights, and progress history that you enter into the App. This data is stored on our servers via Firebase Firestore.

Body Measurement Data
If you choose to log body measurements such as weight, body fat percentage, or body circumferences, this data is stored on our servers via Firebase Firestore.

Usage & Analytics Data
We collect information about how you use the App, including feature interactions, session duration, screen views, and in-app events, via Firebase Analytics.

Device & Technical Data
We may collect your device type, operating system version, app version, and crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics to maintain App stability.

How We Use Your Information
We use collected data to:

Provide, maintain, and improve the App
Personalize your training and recovery experience
Generate AI-powered coaching insights and weekly reports
Process and manage your subscription
Send push notifications you have opted into
Diagnose technical issues and crashes
Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services. Each may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, database storage (Firestore), analytics, and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy

RevenueCat — Subscription management and entitlement tracking. RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Apple App Store — In-app purchases and subscription billing. Apple Privacy Policy

OpenAI — AI coaching responses and weekly report generation. Workout data, body measurements, and profile information are transmitted to OpenAI's API (GPT-4o or equivalent model) to generate personalized responses. We do not use this data for model training. OpenAI Privacy Policy

Supabase — Cloud storage for exercise media content (GIFs and images). Supabase Privacy Policy

Health & Fitness Data
RepRise does not currently integrate with Apple HealthKit. Workout and measurement data you log is stored solely within our service. We do not share health or fitness data with advertisers or use it for targeted advertising purposes.

AI-Generated Content
Certain features of the App use artificial intelligence powered by OpenAI's API. When you use AI coaching or report features, relevant workout and profile data is sent to OpenAI to generate a response. AI-generated content is produced by a third-party language model and may contain inaccuracies. You are interacting with an AI system, not a human coach or medical professional.

Data Retention
We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time using the Delete Account feature within the App. Upon deletion, your data is removed from our active systems. Residual copies in backups may persist for up to 30 days before permanent deletion.

Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase security rules to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
Access the personal data we hold about you
Request correction of inaccurate data
Request deletion of your data (available in-app via Delete Account)
Withdraw consent for optional data processing
Lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at reprise.wt@gmail.com.
